Education & Work Eperience

Throughout my entire adult life, my preliminary goal was to be good at what I do and build a better version of myself in terms of skill sets and knowledge. Yet, there was always this lingering feeling of “I do not belong here” or “my full potential is trapped.” Let me start from the beginning.

Fascinated by other cultures, this journey of mine started by enrolling in a Bachelor’s degree in English Translation, which led me to my first job and eventually becoming an ESL teacher. These experiences allowed me to gain priceless interpersonal skills, adapt to diverse work environments, and, most importantly, developing a creative mind to develop effective solutions.

Despite these achievements, something was missing. I had been so blind of my passion for technology and computer science, even though I used to spend hours and hours behind the computer. In 2022, it hit me to enroll in an online course by HarvardX, Introduction to Computer Science. The time for the big change was already at my doorstep without me even knowing it! I remember the days I struggled for hours, but there was something dragging me along and holding me to that chair; something that made me spent countless hours exploring and learning on my own. That something was THE thing that I was looking for all these years, a true passion. In 2024, I applied for a Master’s degree in Information Technology and graduated with a GPA of 3.7 as the second best in the department. Currently, I am looking forward to finding a position related to this field with excitement.

Hobbies & Interests

Dota 2 is my favorite video game, and it has been a big part of my free time since 2017. Over the years, I have dedicated more than 4,300 hours to playing it, which reflects not only my passion but also the depth of my engagement with the game. I consider myself a true fan, not just of playing, but also of following the competitive scene. Every year, I eagerly watch The International, Dota 2’s biggest tournament, where the best teams from around the world compete for glory, if not for million-dollar prizes. I enjoy supporting my favorite players and teams, analyzing their strategies, and sometimes even trying to apply what I’ve learned to my own gameplay. For me, Dota 2 is more than just a pastime; it is both a source of entertainment and a way to connect with a global community of players and fans.

V for Vendetta, a masterpiece, a philosophy, the world unmasked. This film changed my life forever. It taught me what belief truly means and how deeply we are meant to hold on to our values, even when the world challenges them. I have watched it more than a dozen times, and each viewing feels like the first, bringing me the same excitement and inspiration to the moon and back. To some audiences, it may appear as just another superhero movie with fantasy elements. But to me, it is nothing less than a modern tragedy, a poetic reflection on freedom, identity, and resistance. It captures the art of life and war, reminding us how crucial it is to fight back against oppression and to strive to make things right, even when the odds seem impossible.

Die-cast Model Cars

I believe everyone should have a collection that reflects their personal interests. For me, as a lifelong car enthusiast, collecting die-cast model cars is more than just a hobby, it’s a passion. There’s something deeply satisfying about curating a collection that represents both your taste and your dedication.

When most people hear the word collection, they often imagine rare, high-value items that require significant financial investment. But a collection doesn’t have to be expensive to be meaningful. My own collection consists of models from a variety of brands, many of which are quite affordable. What matters most is the joy and sense of fulfillment I get from expanding it whenever I find a new piece. Over time, each car becomes a story, a memory, and a small reflection of the larger automotive world that fascinates me.
